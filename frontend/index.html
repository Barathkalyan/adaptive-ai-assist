Adaptive AI – Login & Accessibility Setup

Select Accessibility Mode

Use your keyboard (Tab + Enter) or click a button. Blind users will hear instructions automatically.

Login

Enter your phone number and press Enter to login with OTP.

Information

Your choice helps Adaptive AI adapt the interface to your needs.

  • Blind: Screen reader support, TTS guidance, keyboard navigation, high-contrast text
  • Deaf: Large visual buttons, captions, optional vibration feedback
  • Mute: Text input, optional gestures, TTS output for system messages
